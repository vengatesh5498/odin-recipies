Chicken-Biriyani

The Recipe

Chicken Biriyani is one of the famous dishes in the Indian Cousine. It's popularity among the massed have been increasing over time and each region in India have their own version of it. Now, this recipe is based out of southern part of India and we'll dive deep into it's nuances do's and dont's and some trick to nail it. Be it a gathering of friends and family or a simple weekend meal, Chicken Biriyani for sure is a treat to devour!

Ingredients that serves 4

  • Ghee 20ml
  • Peanut Oil 10ml
  • Cardamon, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and star anees
  • Onion 400gms
  • Tomatoes 300gms
  • Ginger and garlic paste 15gms
  • Basmathi Rice 500gms
  • Mint leaves and corriander leaves 30gms each
  • Green chillies 15gms
  • Chicken meat 800gms
  • Grounded Corriander and Chillie
  • Chicken meat 800gms

The preparation

  1. Wash and soak the Basmathi rice in water for 30 minutes
  2. Add in the Ghee and Oil in a pan that can hold a volume of 5 liters and let the oil and gee heat for few minutes
  3. Now add the Cardamon, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and the star anees
  4. Fry the above whole spices and now add in the green chillies with chopped onions
  5. Fry them until the onion turns translucent. Now, add in the ginger-garlic paste to it
  6. After 5 minutes, check if the raw smell of ginger and garlic persists, if not add in the Tomatoes
  7. Let the tomatoes to cook for 10 minutes until they turn mushy
  8. Now add in a couple of spoons (5 to 7 gms) grounded chillie powder and 3 spoons (10 to 12 gms) of grounded corriander and saute it well
  9. After 3 minutes add in the mint and corriander leaves that are finely chopped in
  10. The above mix should now give you a good aroma. It's time to add in the chicken. Mix it well and let the mix cook in medium heat for around 10 to 15 minutes
  11. Meanwhile boil hot water in a pan. This is an important step where you need to keep track of the proportion. A rule of thumb, add in 150ml of water to every 100gms of rice
  12. Add in the soaked rice by dripping off any excess water. Give it a good mix along with all the ingredients in the pan and add the boiling water
  13. Add in salt to taste, 12-15gms would do for this serving
  14. Close the pan with a lid and let it cook for 25 minutes under low heat
  15. After 25 minutes, turn off the heat and leave it for 20 more minutes.
  16. Vola! Your Biriyani is now ready.
